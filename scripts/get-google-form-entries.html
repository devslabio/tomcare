Instructions:

  1. Open your Google Form in a new tab
  2. Right-click on the form and select "Inspect" or press F12
  3. In the Elements/Inspector tab, press Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) to search
  4. Search for entry. - you'll see entries like name="entry.123456789"
  5. Note down the entry IDs in order (first field = first entry ID, etc.)
  6. Alternatively, submit a test response and check the Google Sheets response - column headers show entry IDs
